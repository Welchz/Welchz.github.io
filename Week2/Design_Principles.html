Design Principles Document

Lyft

Lyft.com

Lyft is a good example of visual hierarchy. They put the driver signup tab at the top of the screen, and further down they place it above the rider app option. The company values its drivers as they are the main part of the company, without drivers there can't be any passengers.

Apple

Apple.com

Apple is a good example of the contrast principle. The site has a very basic color layout, with pictures of the devices that pop out at you due to the stark difference between the white background and the colorful screens. Each different device has a similar page setup

Reddit

Reddit.com

Reddit is a good example of the repitition principle of P.A.R.C.. Each post has the same top bar setup and the same bottom bar setup. Pictures all appear on the left and links will appear below the post title.
